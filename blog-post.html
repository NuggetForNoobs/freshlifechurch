    Life Missions

    Sunday Service from July 13, 2025
    Pastor
    Franky Rewah
    Copied ✔


    Life often feels like a storm we can’t escape, yet the message of the Gospel is that God meets us right in the middle of the chaos. Jesus reminds us that even when the waves rise high, He is the anchor that keeps us from drifting away. In every trial we face, His presence brings comfort and guidance, reminding us that we are never truly alone.

    Our strength is not in what we can control, but in the One who has already overcome the world. Hope in Christ is not a fragile wish but a firm foundation, a promise that no matter how dark the night becomes, we are held. It is in surrendering our fears and trusting Him fully that we find peace that surpasses understanding, and courage to face the challenges ahead.

    Let us commit to living out our calling—serving the church, loving the lost, and proclaiming the good news with boldness and humility. Every act of kindness, every word of encouragement, and every step of faith is a testimony to His love working through us. Even in moments of doubt or weakness, God’s grace empowers us to persevere and shine His light to those around us.

    Remember that our journey is not measured by the absence of struggle but by the presence of faith. Each challenge can teach us patience, resilience, and compassion. By leaning into God’s guidance and listening for His voice, we discover clarity and purpose amidst the noise of life. Our mission is to embody His love, extend mercy, and offer hope to a world desperately in need of both.

    May we hold fast to the truths we have received, nurturing our spirit through prayer, Scripture, and fellowship. Let our lives reflect the Gospel in action—through forgiveness, humility, and unwavering trust. As we walk this path, may our hearts remain steadfast, our minds focused, and our souls anchored in the eternal promises of Christ, who strengthens us for every season.
